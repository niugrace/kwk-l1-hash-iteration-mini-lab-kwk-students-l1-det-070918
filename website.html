here is the labrador retriever!

the Labrador Retriever, or just Labrador, is a type of retriever-gun dog. the Labrador is one of the most popular breeds of dog in Canada, the United Kingdom and the United States.


here is the yorkshire terrier!

the Yorkshire Terrier is a small dog breed of terrier type, developed during the 19th century in Yorkshire, England, to catch rats in clothing mills.


here is the german shepherd!

the German Shepherd is a breed of medium to large-sized working dog that originated in Germany. the breed's officially recognized name is German Shepherd Dog in the English language. the breed was once known as the Alsatian in Britain and Ireland.


here is the maltese!

personally one of my favorite breeds, the maltese, Canis familiaris Maelitacus, is a small breed of dog in the Toy Group. it descends from dogs originating in the Central Mediterranean Area.


here is the french bulldog!

another one of my favorite breeds, the French Bulldog, also known as Frenchie, is a small breed of domestic dog. frenchies were the result in the 1800s of a cross between bulldog ancestors imported from England and local ratters in Paris, France.
